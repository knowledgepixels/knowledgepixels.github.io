Knowledge Pixels Incubator

Our incubator program is where new use cases around nanopublications can start and grow.

What we are looking for

We are looking for individuals interested in:

  • Making the first steps of applying the nanopublication ecosystem to their use case
  • Exploring ways to obtain funding to implement their use case through co-creation with Knowledge Pixels

What you get

As part of the incubator program you will receive:

  • Supervision and guidance
  • Weekly office hours with our developers
  • Prioritized feature requests
  • mMaterials to help you pitch your use case
  • Visibility of your use case via our website, Nanodash, and other channels

How to apply

To apply, send a short paragraph describing your use case, motivation, organization (if applicable), and potential funding opportunities to info@knowledgepixels.com.
