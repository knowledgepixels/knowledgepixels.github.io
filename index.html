The future of knowledge

We aim to kick-start the next revolution in scientific publishing and knowledge sharing. Using our core technology, nanopublications, we are building a global knowledge ecosystem to make humanity's knowledge radically more efficient and effective. Our work is guided by openness and freedom to operate.

Nanopublications

Nanopublications are a format consisting of tiny snippets of information about any topic whatsoever that is encoded in a way that computers can understand and humans can easily create. Nanopublications allow researchers to access the findings of their fields with their relevant contexts as a connected global knowledge graph.

Our services, ready for you

We provide cutting-edge managed services to browse, publish, and query the global knowledge graph made of nanopublications. We have user-friendly UIs for humans, as well as powerful APIs for machines. They all connect to an ecosystem that is fully open, semantic, decentralized, and aligned with the FAIR principles.
